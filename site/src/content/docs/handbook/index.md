## About the game

Escape the Valley is an Oregon Trail-style survival game that runs in your terminal. Lead a party of settlers through procedurally generated wilderness. Manage food, water, wagon condition, and morale while navigating events, hazards, and hard choices.

An optional AI Game Master (powered by Ollama) narrates your journey. An optional XRPL Testnet ledger tracks your supplies on-chain.
